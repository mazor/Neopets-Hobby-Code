Look at this page for <a href="#Deadlines">deadlines</a>, <a href="#Rules">rules</a>, <a href="#Applying">how to apply</a>, <a href="#Voting">how to vote</a>, and the entries (<a href="#Category1">cute</a>, <a href="#Category2">creative</a>, and <a href="#Category3">casual</a>). <h2>Deadlines</h2>
<p>Applications start on the 29th of July
</p><p>Applications close on the 14th of August 6am NST
</p><p>Voting begins on the 15th of August
</p><p>Voting ends on the <strike>22nd</strike> 29th of August 11:30pm NST
</p><p>Winners crowned on the <strike>23rd</strike> 30th of August
<a name="Rules"></a>
</p><h2>General Rules and Regulations</h2>
<ol><li>MOST IMPORTANTLY, be nice please! Anyone being intentionally horrible will be kicked out the competition (and possibly from the guild if Becki deems it bad enough)</li>
<li>No buying votes (this includes: gifts of services, voting for their pet expecting a vote back, NP, items) as this then becomes a competition on who has the most to spend rather than the best outfit </li>
<li>Constructive criticism can be very useful and may even  be welcome but ensure IT IS before giving it :)</li>
<li>Deadlines are final but applications may be changed up to the deadline</li>
<li>Have fun!</li>
</ol><a name="Applying"></a>
<h2>Applying to Enter</h2>
<h3>Rules:</h3>
<ol><li>The outfit must be on your pet when you apply and must remain there until the picture is on this page (a neomail will be sent to you to let you know if you can change it) </li>
<li>All applications are done via neomail to the host</li>
<li>You may enter multiple pets (one entry per pet per category, up to 5 entries per category*)</li>
<li>You may enter the same outfit for multiple categories even if it is on the same pet :)</li>
</ol><p>You may change applications up to the deadline but the above rules remain! If you reapply with the same pet in a category you've already applied for, the outfit will be OVERRIDDEN so make sure that is what you want.
</p><p>You may advertise your pet as much as you want once the outfit is on here (the host will also let the guild know of changes each time an outfit is added or overriden)
</p><p>You may include an outfit name/description (optional) with your application to explain the outfit but either make it under 40 characters or post it on the guild board instead :) An example would be 'Beach Buddy'
</p><p> Neomail<a href="http://www.neopets.com/neomessages.phtml?type=send&recipient=dont_earn_np_on_side"> dont_earn_np_on_side</a> with the following example neomail:
<br></p><center>
Subject: My SummerFest Competition Application
<br>
Message: 
<br>
Category 1 - pet name (Description), pet2 name (Description)
<br>
Category 3 - pet3 name, pet4 name
<br></center>
<br>
Your applications may require several neomails as the outfit must still be active when I view it as that is how I get the pictures and prove you had the outfit.
<p>*Side Account pets are acceptable. Max 5 rule is per person not per account. So you may have for example 3 from your main and 2 from your side.
</p>

<a name="Voting"></a>
<h2>Voting</h2>
<h3>Rules:</h3>
<ol><li>You may only vote once per category </li>
<li>You cannot vote for your own pet (where would be the fun in that? :P) </li>
<li>All votes are done by neomailing the host </li>
<li>All votes are anonymous throughout the competition. If you want to share the pet you voted for either neomail the owner of that pet (but do not do it expecting a vote back, see rule 2 in General Rules and Regulations) or wait until the winner has been announced </li><li>The host can vote but not enter their pets to be voted on </li>
</ol><p> This competition works best if you vote based on the pet not the owner but it's your vote :)
</p><p> Neomail<a href="http://www.neopets.com/neomessages.phtml?type=send&recipient=dont_earn_np_on_side"> dont_earn_np_on_side</a> with the following example neomail:
<br></p><center>Subject: My Summerfest Competition Votes</center>
<br><center>Message: 
<br>
Category 1 - pet name
<br>
 
Category 2 - pet name 
<br>

Category 3 - pet name 
<br></center>
<p>The pet doesn't need to be different in each category but make sure to vote for your favourite in each category! :D
